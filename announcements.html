Paper Pattern

  • Business & Society
    • Total 4 Questions (All Long)
    • 1 Question = 1 page answer only (2 sides of a page) Exceeding the requirement will lead to negative marking. State to the point.
    • Sample Questions: State the concept and corroborate with real life case work. Or, differentiate between two concepts etc.
    • Case Works examples may find in slides and slides' notes
    • Case Work, Case Study, Example - synonyms ans to your questions where needed YES
  • Quantitative Reasoning
    • Time: 90 minutes
    • No MCQs
    • Conceptual Short Questions (what's this, what's that? etc)
    • No Extra Sheet - On the Paper Questions
    • Long + Short Combo
    • No Choice
    • Hint: Exercise Questions (some are as it is)
    • Long Question (10 Marks / 3 + 4 + 3 for a, b , c parts respectively)
    • No sharing is allowed. Bring your own calculator.
  • Microeconomics
    • Time: 1 hour
    • 4 Questions - 3 Labeled Diagrams + 1 Theoretical
    • Theoretical: 15 Marks - 5 Short Questions - Definitions + Examples
    • Diagrams Hint: Circular Flow Diagram, Production Possibility Curve/Frontier, Midpoint Formula Questions (Structure: Interpret answer + Show in diagram), Shifts in Supply & Demand Curve
    • Short Question Examples: Price Ceiling, Price Floor, Minimum Wage (concept) etc
    • Questions could be hypothetical
  • Functional English
    • Short + Long Questions
    • Creative Writing / Essay Writing with words limit
    • Evaluation based on Language Accessibility
    • Correcting Grammatical Error Passages
  • Applications of ICT
    • 10 Short Questions (20 Marks)
    • 1 Long Question (10 Marks)
